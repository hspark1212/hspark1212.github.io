Materials.AI

I'm Hyunsoo Park.

I'm a postdoc researcher in Prof. Aron Walsh's team at Imperial College London. I received my PhD degree at KAIST, under guidance of Prof. Jihan Kim.
I have strong interests in developing computational tools incorporating AI and machine learning models for materials design, including structure-property relationship, inverse design, and high-throughput screening.
Feel free to explore my Github profile!
